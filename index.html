Dominik Filipiak

Majored in Computer Science, now a PhD student (Quantitative Economics) – a mixture of technical experience and theoretical background. Has hands-on experience with collecting data from scratch, cleansing things, building ML models, transferring them to code and inferring new findings. Interested in doing new and exciting things connected to Data Science, Machine/Deep Learning, and Big Data, as well as in Arts & Humanities. Preferably in Python or R.

Keywords: Artifical Intelligence, Cultural Economics, Machince Learning, Digital Humanities, Quantitative Analysis
